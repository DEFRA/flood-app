Flood risk

Give your consent before using the flood information service prototype

Thank you for taking part in our research. We’d like you to look at a prototype service we’re working on. You can use it in exactly the same way and for the same reasons you would use the real service.

This prototype uses real data to provide a picture of flood risk in any area you select.

We’d like to know about your experience as you use it. Tell us about it using the feedback button you should see on the right hand side of your screen, or by emailing us. What makes your experience good, or not so good? What frustrates you? What’s missing? How does it make you feel?

Be honest. We want to understand what the service needs to look like and what it needs to do. Everything we learn will be used to streamline the design and improve the usability of the service.

Before you start

By proceeding you are consenting to our use of cookies. These are small files we place onto your computer to collect information about how you browse the service.

Find out more about cookies on GOV.UK and how to manage cookies. You can also learn more about how we use cookies and related technologies by reading our privacy policy and the Hotjar privacy policy.

Select ‘Consent and go to prototype' to proceed.
